Santa Cruz

Santa Cruz is a city in Bolivia, known for its rich cultural attractions. One of the most popular tourist sites is the cathedral located in the central square, "24 de Septiembre." Many visitors are drawn to this cathedral for its architectural beauty and historical significance. As the most emblematic building in the city, it stands out both for its size and its storied past, which dates back to the 16th century when the first stone was laid.
